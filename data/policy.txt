===============================
TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
===============================

1. Hotel
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹5000 per day
- Receipt is mandatory.

2. Meal
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹1000 per day
- Receipt is mandatory.

3. Taxi
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹1500 per day
- Receipt is mandatory.

4. Flight
- Only Economy Class tickets are eligible.
- Business Class and First Class are not reimbursable.
- Receipt is mandatory.

5. Train
- AC Chair Car and Sleeper Class are reimbursable.
- Receipt is mandatory.

6. Manager Approval
- Claims above ₹10,000 require manager approval.
- If manager approval is missing, send the claim for Manual Review.

7. Duplicate Claims
- Duplicate reimbursement claims are not allowed.
- If a duplicate claim is detected, reject the claim.

8. Missing Documents
- If a mandatory receipt is missing, reject the claim.
- If supporting documents are incomplete, send for Manual Review.

9. Eligible Categories
- Hotel
- Meal
- Taxi
- Flight
- Train

10. Non-Eligible Categories
- Personal shopping
- Entertainment
- Alcohol
- Family expenses
- Personal gifts

11. Decision Rules

Approve
- Receipt available
- Amount within policy limit
- Eligible category
- No duplicate
- Manager approval available (if required)

Partially Approve
- Amount exceeds policy limit
- Approve only the maximum allowed amount

Reject
- Receipt missing
- Duplicate claim
- Non-eligible category

Manual Review
- Missing manager approval
- Conflicting information
- Policy exception
